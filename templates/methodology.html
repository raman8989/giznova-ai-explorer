📊 Giznova AI Explorer Methodology

Giznova AI Explorer rankings are based on publicly available device specifications and AI platform information.

How Rankings Are Calculated

  • NPU TOPS performance
  • AI platform capabilities
  • On-device AI support
  • Processor and NPU architecture
  • AI ecosystem support

Data Sources

  • Official manufacturer specifications
  • Chipset vendor documentation
  • Public AI platform documentation
  • Device launch information

Important Notes

  • Higher TOPS does not always guarantee better real-world AI performance.
  • Software optimization can significantly impact AI features.
  • Actual AI performance may vary by device, operating system, and application.
  • Rankings are provided for informational purposes only.

Disclaimer
